# Brave Prior Art

Brave is a Chromium-based browser with native privacy and ad-blocking features.
It is useful public prior art for understanding:

- Chromium-based product maintenance.
- browser-level content blocking.
- Windows packaging and update concerns.
- privacy-focused product expectations.

Blank Browser is a separate product and does not aim to copy Brave's product
surface, rewards system, wallet, or branding.
